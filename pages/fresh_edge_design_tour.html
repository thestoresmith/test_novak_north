About the Project

Fresh Edge on Demand was in the market to have their e-commerce store migrated over to Shopify. An important aspect the client requested was having an online storefront that isn't too overwhelming. They wanted to keep it simple in order to keep the attention on their featured product, the at home skate sharpener. The client did want the online store to have the ability to incorporate their affiliate marketing program. This was accomplished, but did require some custom liquid code. With our expert skill set, we were able to work with the client to find a Shopify theme that fit their budget, but also provided a solution to their requests.

Key Points

  • Debut Shopify theme
  • This theme was selected for it's simplicity
  • Client didn't want site to take away from key product beign sold
  • Some limitations of the theme were avoided by modifying theme code
  • Custom logo design
  • Custom Instagram section on homepage
  • Chanded background color of testimonials to make the section stand out
  • Dynamic "BUY IT NOW BUTTON"
  • Client requested custom affiiate product page
  • Utilized custom code for affiliate product page hide everything besides product info and "See on Amazon" button
